Rhythmical Massage Therapy Association China (PC)

Missions

  • Establish a platform of study and communication to those Rhythmical Massage Therapists who devote to maintain profession and development
  • To encourage the development of the Rhythmical Massage Therapy Profession in China

Aims

SUSTAINED:Sustained contact, sustained study, Sustained development

PROFESSIONAL: To become a group of supportive colleagues who in turn could help and encourage each other

QUALITY: To further professional skills and knowledge

RIGHTS

  • List in the Association Website, more people can find and know you;
  • Full Member can participate in the post graduated course;
  • Trainings or other events organized by Full Member could be issued in the Association Website after the approval of the Council;
  • Criticism, advice and supervision rights on Association;
  • Free will of join or quit.

DUTIES

  • Obey the rules and resolutions of Association;
  • Defend the interests and reputations of Association;
  • Promise a sustained study, including but not limited to post graduated course and related events organized by the Association;
  • provide valid registration information;
  • The timely payment of dues and complete the work assigned by the Association.
